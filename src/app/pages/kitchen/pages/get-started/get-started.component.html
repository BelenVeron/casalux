Our design process

Excellent you are ready to have an amazing kitchen done. In order to do this, you will have to select from the hundreds of finishes we have the COLORS you like. The tab OPENING SYSTEMS is where you have to tell us if you want a kitchen with handles, a Grove, or a completely minimal kitchen where you just touch to open. T COLLECTIONS tab is the place where you select the styles of kitchens you like the most, and the ones you find interesting. In the tab for CABINET OPTIONS, you can select the types of cabinets that you want to see and the options that are important to you. We will be using these options to guide us on what is important for you.

After you select these options you will be directed to a section where you will decide if you want to have CasaLux go to your house and get all the dimensions for the space and go over with other selections. This option has a cost of $500 and it is not refundable. Or you can select the option to draw your sketch and provide the appliances that you like. Once you finish with this CasaLux will start the design process, this has a cost of $1000 that will be applied to the kitchen cabinet order.
